D-Lay
Delay plugin

Digital delay gives you a wide range of time manipulation from modelling classic tape delay for vocals and drums to endless echoes in breaks of your dance hit!

  • Independent or linked delay lines for each chanel
  • Up to 2000ms delay time with 0.01ms accuracy in time mode or up to 2 bars in host-sync mode
  • BandPass filter with adjustable Q
  • Stereo image turning with Time Shift knobs for each channel and Master Shift knob for both channels
